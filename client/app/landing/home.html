Simplified Project Management Systems

Simple

SPMS simplifies project management for the small to medium devlopment companies. Helping to increase work efficiency and reduce costs via helipng reduce wasted time in development.

Efficient

By providing the correct information to Project Managers and their team SPMS will increasse their efficiency. this is accomplished through the use of Graphics and data presented in a visually appelaing. productive way.

Intuitive

SPMS aims to be intuitive to all users to help enforce the 2 previous points of Being simple and efficient. this is achieved by having a clear layout of the dashboards and by only showing data that is needed by that user.

What Is SPMS?

SPMS was a 3rd year project from the University of Wollongong. On delivery of the final project it was suggested that we take it public and since then we have been working hard to restructure the product into an appropriate systems for use by Clients.
